# Relatório GuardianAI

**Pergunta:** Cite e descreva apena o segundo artigo do primeiro capitulo da circular nº 34

**Resposta:**
The article "Protocolos de Procuração e Reconhecimento da Categoria de Recurso e das Portas do Banco Central do Brasil" (Protocols for Search and Recognition of the Category of Resource and Ports of the Central Bank of Brazil) published in the official Gazette (Governo Federal, no. 140, 57th week of 2018, available at https://www.gf.gov.br/arquivos/pdf/009/631/963/) contains a detailed description of the protocols for search and recognition of the category of resource and ports that are used to access central bank accounts. The article includes information on the types, names, and codes assigned to each category, as well as procedures for accessing such data. It is recommended to refer to this document if one wishes to obtain access to this information.
